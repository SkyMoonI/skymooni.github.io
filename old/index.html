Who am I?

In this section of my website I will talk a little bit about myself.

Hey there! I'm Gokay Iseri, a recent Computer Engineering graduate with a serious passion for game development. Since my university days, I’ve been on a journey to turn my love for games into something real—working on various game projects, learning from online resources like YouTube tutorials, Unity Learn, and Udemy courses, and just diving deep into the world of Unity development.

Over the last year, I’ve focused on building my skills by creating small games where I’ve implemented cool mechanics like object pooling, player input systems, and even some procedural level generation. Coding in C# has become my thing, and I’m constantly learning how to make my projects more efficient, optimized, and fun to play.

But I’m not just about coding! I also dabble in 3D modeling with Blender, where I’ve created low-poly assets that give my games a unique visual touch. Understanding how the art and technical sides of game development come together has really expanded my perspective.

My Motto

“Keep learning, keep improving.” I believe that the best way to grow is by constantly challenging yourself and always staying curious. There’s always something new to learn, and that’s what makes this journey so exciting!

Currently learning with Unity Learn - Creative Core.
