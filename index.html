About Me

Hi! I'm Gökay İşeri, a passionate developer with a background in Computer Engineering from Sakarya University. During my university years, I explored various areas of software development, with a strong focus on game development, alongside mobile and web development projects.

I also spent time learning 3D modeling in Blender to support my game design efforts, combining creativity with technical skills. After graduation, I continued to pursue game development, working on personal projects and deepening my skills in Unity and C#.

However, challenges in finding industry opportunities led me to shift my focus toward software engineering, starting with web development. Currently, I'm expanding my skills further by following Dr. Angela Yu's Web Development Bootcamp, building a solid foundation in full-stack web technologies and modern development practices.

I enjoy combining creativity with technical skills—whether in games, interactive websites, or software tools—and I'm always looking forward to learning and growing as a developer.
